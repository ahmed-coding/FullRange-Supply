## 5. CONTENT OPTIMIZATION GUIDELINES

### Blog Post Structure (Standard Template):

```
TITLE (60-70 chars)
Meta Description (155-160 chars)

INTRO (100-150 words)
- Hook/problem statement
- What reader will learn
- Why it matters

H2: First Main Section
- 2-3 paragraphs (300-400 words)
- Include 1-2 keywords naturally
- Use bullet points for scannability

H2: Second Main Section
- 2-3 paragraphs (300-400 words)
- Include 1-2 keywords
- Real examples/statistics

H2: Third Main Section
[Same pattern...]

H2: Common Mistakes / FAQ
- 4-6 Q&A items
- Quick answers
- Internal links

H2: Key Takeaways
- 5-7 bullet points
- Action items
- Next steps

CTA: Free Consultation / Download
- "Next Steps" section
- Lead generation focused

```

### Keyword Placement Best Practices:

| Element | Keyword | Placement | Quantity |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Title | Primary | First 60 chars | 1 |
| Meta Description | Primary/Secondary | First 160 chars | 1-2 |
| H1 | Primary | Once only | 1 |
| First 100 words | Primary | Natural usage | 1-2 |
| H2 headings | Secondary | 4-6 headings | 4-6 |
| Body text | Various | Throughout | 15-20 total |
| Image alt text | Related keywords | Each image | 1 per image |
| URL slug | Primary | Domain/title | 1 |

### Internal Linking Strategy:

**Per Article Links:** 3-5 internal links

Examples:
- "Learn more about [service page]"
- "Read our guide on [related topic]"
- "See our case study on [similar project]"
- "Check our FAQ on [related question]"

**Link Anchor Text:** Keyword-rich but natural
- ❌ Bad: "click here"
- ❌ Bad: "AMZ Global Sourcing"
- ✓ Good: "China supplier verification guide"
- ✓ Good: "complete sourcing checklist"
